To know more

Opening a Kotak811 zero balance online is extremely easy and takes just a few minutes. You can opt for a video KYC process and open a full-fledged savings account within a few hours! Enter your name, mobile number, and email ID. Tick the checkboxes if you are not a politically exposed person and you are an Indian resident. You will receive an OTP on your mobile number. Enter the OTP. As per RBIs regulation, banks can open zero balance savings account online through Aadhaar OTP verification. Enter your PAN and Aadhaar number. You need to authorize the bank to access your Aadhaar details and biometric information for verification. Add the OTP that you have received on your Aadhaar registered mobile number. You can now see your address as per your Aadhaar records. Use this address or add another address as your communication address. Add details such as the fathers and mothers name, occupation, gender and annual income. The bank will fetch your date of birth from the Aadhaar database. You can also add nominee details.
